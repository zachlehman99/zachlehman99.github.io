This is my first blog

This is the first time I have ever thought about doing this, but it just feels right. I say that because I really like putting my thoughts out there in writing. I would not say I am the greatest at it, but it is fun and it is a release of whatever is on your mind.

My intial thought on blogging was I can't do this and I will never be able to do this and yet I am still attempting this. I really do not want this to be drawn out or anything like that, but I do want it to be a place where I can share my thoughts.

This first blog will be short and sweet. I hope whoever is reading this will wait and come back in the near future to read an actual blog. It will definently be way more interesting than me just telling you what I will be doing. I hope to see you back for blog number 2.
